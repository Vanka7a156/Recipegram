About RecipeGram

RecipeGram is a web platform designed for sharing and discovering homemade recipes from people all around the world. Whether you're a passionate home cook or just learning to boil water, RecipeGram gives you the space to showcase your cooking, explore new dishes, and connect with others through food.

Users can create accounts, upload recipes with images, like others' recipes, and leave comments to share thoughts or suggestions. RecipeGram brings the joy of cooking into a friendly and interactive online community.

Our mission is to inspire creativity in the kitchen and make recipe sharing as easy and enjoyable as possible.
